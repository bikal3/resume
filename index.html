GPS technology on public transportation Research | IOT | Oracle

github.com/bikal3/BusSewa
One of my side projects
The project focused on the research and implementation of GPS technology in public transportation within the context of Nepal’s unique infrastructure challenges. It highlighted the complexities of adopting basic technologies, commonly seen in other countries, in the Nepalese setting due to factors like inconsistent network coverage, lack of standardization in vehicle registration, and limited technological literacy among stakeholders. The study involved assessing the feasibility of GPS integration for real-time vehicle tracking, route optimization, and passenger convenience, while addressing issues such as affordability and system scalability. The findings underscored the need for tailored solutions that align with local conditions to modernize Nepal’s public transportation system effectively.
